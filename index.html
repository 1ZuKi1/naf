Who we are

Nomadic heritage,
modern agriculture.

Nuudelchin Agro Farm LLC is an independent Mongolian dairy and livestock company established in 2018 with a clear mission: to modernize the country's dairy sector. That year, we opened Mongolia's first 400-cow cluster dairy farm — a model that united 10 herder families under one sustainable system. Since then, we've expanded to three successful clusters, imported over 450 Montbéliarde cattle from France, and are now building our largest farm yet in Ugtaaltsaidam. We haven't stopped since, and we're just getting started.

Mission

To develop intensive livestock farming in Mongolia and build resilient, sustainable value chains rooted in agricultural resources — from farm planning and construction to processing and market integration.

Vision

To bring Mongolia's dairy sector to international standards by producing healthy, safe, and high-quality raw materials that protect public health.

Our farms — Töv province

Four clusters,
one steppe.

Each cluster farm unites 10 herder households around shared infrastructure, veterinary care, and a guaranteed milk market. Every family works 10 hectares of its own land — 2 for planting, 8 preserved as pasture — and together the clusters supply 900,000 liters of milk to domestic dairy factories every year.

Recognition

A national movement,
a presidential visit.

On December 10, 2024, His Excellency Khurelsukh Ukhnaa, President of Mongolia, visited Nuudelchin Agro Farm's operations within the framework of the "Food Supply and Security" National Movement — briefed on our achievements, milk production, and plans for future expansion.

"If this incentive is provided throughout the year, it will be a strong impetus for herder cooperatives to expand their operation."

— J. Battugs, Director, Nuudelchin Agro Farm LLC

"We aim to import 1,000 purebred cows for milk and meat, and request government support for creating a select herd by 2030."

— J. Battugs, Director, Nuudelchin Agro Farm LLC

A herder's story
